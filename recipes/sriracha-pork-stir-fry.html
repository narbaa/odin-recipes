Sriracha Pork Stir-Fry

with Cabbage, Zesty Rice & Peanuts

Description

Ready to stir up your meal routine? This mouthwatering dish is guaranteed to turn some heads in the best possible way, starting with that signature stir-fry sizzle. Pork and cabbage are browned, then simmered in a spicy-sweet sauce and layered over lime rice. It’s all sprinkled with scallions, drizzled with Sriracha, and finished with crunchy peanuts. We just can’t take our eyes (and forks) off this dinner—and frankly, we don’t want to!

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork - 10 oz
  • Red Cabbage and Carrot Mix - 1 bag
  • Sweet Soy Glaze - 1 bag
  • Lime - 1
  • Jasmine Rice - 0.75 cup
  • Scallions - 2
  • Peanuts - 1 bag
  • Sriracha - 1 packet
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Cooking oil - 1 drizzle
  • Salt, Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry produce. In a small pot, combine rice, 1¼ cups water (2¼ cups for 4 servings), and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a low simmer. Cook until rice is tender, 15-20 minutes. Keep covered off heat until ready to serve.
  2. While rice cooks, zest and quarter lime. Trim and thinly slice scallions, separating whites from greens. Peel and finely chop garlic. Roughly chop peanuts.
  3. In a small bowl, combine soy glaze, half the Sriracha (save the rest for serving), and juice from half the lime.
  4. Heat a drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add scallion whites and garlic; cook, stirring, until fragrant, 30 seconds. Add pork and season with salt and pepper. Cook, breaking up meat into pieces, until pork is browned and cooked through, 4-6 minutes. Add cabbage and carrot mix and stir until slightly softened, 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add soy glaze mixture and 1 TBSP butter (2 TBSP for 4 servings) to pan. Stir until sauce has thickened and stir-fry is evenly coated, 1-2 minutes. Taste and season with salt and pepper if desired. Turn off heat.
  6. Fluff rice with a fork; stir in lime zest and 1 TBSP butter (2 TBSP for 4 servings). Divide rice between bowls. Top with stir-fryand sprinkle with peanuts and scallion greens. Drizzle with as much remaining Sriracha as you like. Serve with remaining lime wedges on the side.
